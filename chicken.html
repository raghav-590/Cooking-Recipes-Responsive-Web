Chicken Curry

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Chicken (500g)
  • Two onions (sliced)
  • One tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • One tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Two green chilies
  • One tsp turmeric powder
  • One tsp coriander powder
  • One tsp cumin seeds
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish.

Instructions

  • Heat oil and crackle cumin seeds; sauté onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes; cook until soft.
  • Add chicken and dry spices; sear the meat on high heat.
  • Add one cup of water, cover, and cook until chicken is tender.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander.

Additional Information

  • Category : Non-Veg
  • Preparation Time : 45 minutes
  • Difficulty Level : Medium
