Paneer Butter Masala

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes (250g)
  • Two large tomatoes (pureed)
  • One onion (finely chopped)
  • One tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Cashews (soaked and ground)
  • Two tbsp butter
  • One tsp red chili powder
  • Half tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cream for garnish.

Instructions

  • Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until translucent.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  • Stir in the cashew paste and spices.
  • Add paneer cubes and a little water, simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with cream and serve hot.

Additional Information

  • Category : Veg
  • Preparation Time : 30 minutes
  • Difficulty Level : Medium
