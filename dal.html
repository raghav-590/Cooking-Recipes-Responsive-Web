Dal Tadka

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Toor dal (one cup)
  • Two cups water
  • One tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • Two cloves garlic (chopped)
  • Half tsp turmeric powder
  • One tsp cumin seeds
  • Two dried red chilles
  • One tsp ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish.

Instructions

  • Pressure cook the dal with water, turmeric, and salt until soft.
  • In a small pan, heat ghee for the 'Tadka'.
  • Add cumin seeds, garlic, and red chilies until they brown slightly.
  • Pour the hot tempering over the cooked dal and cover immediately to trap the aroma.

Additional Information

  • Category : Veg
  • Preparation Time : 25 minutes
  • Difficulty Level : Easy
