Sarson ka Saag

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Green mustard (500g)
  • Spinach (500g)
  • Bathua (500g)
  • Two medium onions (chopped)
  • Three tsp maize flour
  • Two tsp ghee
  • One tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Pinch of asafoetida

Instructions

  • Thoroughly wash all the leafy greens and chop them finely.
  • Place the greens in a pressure cooker with green chilies, salt, and a little water. Cook for about 4-5 whistles until very soft.
  • Once cooled, mash the greens into a coarse paste using a traditional wooden masher (mathni) or a blender for a smoother texture.
  • Mix the maize flour with a little water to make a paste and stir it into the cooked greens. Simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee. Add hing, onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until golden brown.
  • Pour this tempering over the saag, stir well, and simmer for another 5 minutes. Serve hot with white butter and Makki ki Roti.

Additional Information

  • Category : Veg
  • Preparation Time : 60 minutes
  • Difficulty Level : Hard
