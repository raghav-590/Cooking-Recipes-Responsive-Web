Masala Omlette

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Toor eggs
  • Two small onions (chopped)
  • One green chilles
  • One tsp butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish.

Instructions

  • Whisk eggs in a bowl with onions, chilies, and all spices.
  • Heat butter in a non-stick frying pan.
  • Pour the egg mixture and spread evenly.
  • Cook for 2 minutes, flip, and cook the other side until golden.

Additional Information

  • Category : Non-Veg
  • Preparation Time : 10 minutes
  • Difficulty Level : Easy
